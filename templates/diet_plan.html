Understand your needs

The daily calorie intake that you saw before was for maintaining your body weight. Now we will look at how to reduce body fat.

There are many diets that can be followed but we will look at only the Low carb diet because it is ideal for first-timers.

Low Carb diet can be used by anyone new but takes some time to show results. It includes taking in 40 % carbohydrate, 30% protein and 30% fat.

How many calories should you intake as you start your diet? Reduce 150 caories from your required daily intake for the first week and every week reduce a further 50.
